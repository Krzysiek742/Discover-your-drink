Cuba Libre

Ingredients:

  • ½ lime.
  • 50ml rum, we used white rum.
  • 100ml cola.
  • ice.

Instructions:

  1. Grab a frozen highball glass.
  2. Start off by adding in the freshly squeezed lime juice and a healthy measure of rum. Then fill the glass with lots of ice.
  3. Top up with cola and stir again, gently this time so as not to lose the bubbles. Garnish with a lime wedge and enjoy your Cuba Libre! >

Hustory As the story goes, in the year 1900, a captain in the U.S. Army stationed in Havana during the Spanish-American War poured Coca-Cola and a squeeze of lime into his Bacardí and toasted his Cuban comrades by calling out in the bar, “Por Cuba Libre!” (“To a free Cuba!”). And just like that, a legend was born.
