Old Cuban

Ingredients:

  • 45 ml rum (preferably aged rum, such as Cuban rum)
  • 15 ml fresh lime juice
  • 15 ml simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • 60 ml champagne or sparkling wine

Instructions:

  1. In a cocktail shaker, muddle the mint leaves gently to release their aroma..
  2. Add rum, lime juice, simple syrup, and bitters to the shaker
  3. Fill the shaker with ice and shake well to combine the ingredients.
  4. Strain the mixture into a chilled cocktail glass
  5. Top the drink with champagne or sparkling wine.
  6. Garnish with a mint sprig.

Note:Enjoy your refreshing Old Cuban cocktail!
