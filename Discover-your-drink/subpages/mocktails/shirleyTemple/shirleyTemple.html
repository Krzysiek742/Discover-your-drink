Shirley Temple

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup ginger ale
  • 1/2 cup lemon-lime soda (such as Sprite or 7UP)
  • 2 tablespoons grenadine syrup
  • ice cubes
  • cherries (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes. This will help keep your Shirley Temple chilled.
  2. Pour the ginger ale and lemon-lime soda into the glass. You can adjust the quantities to suit your taste preferences.
  3. Slowly pour the grenadine syrup over the soda mixture. The grenadine will sink to the bottom and create a lovely red hue. Feel free to adjust the amount of grenadine based on your desired level of sweetness.
  4. Give the drink a gentle stir to mix the grenadine throughout the soda.
  5. Garnish your Shirley Temple with a maraschino cherry or two. You can also add a splash of the cherry syrup from the jar for added flavor.
  6. Serve your Shirley Temple chilled and enjoy!

Note: Note: You can also add a squeeze of fresh lime or lemon juice for a tangy twist if you prefer.

Remember, the recipe can be adjusted according to your personal taste preferences, so feel free to experiment and make it your own. Cheers!
