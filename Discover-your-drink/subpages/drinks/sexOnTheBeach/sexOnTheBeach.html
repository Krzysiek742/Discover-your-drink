Sex on The Beach

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 ounces vodka
  • 1/2 ounce peach schnapps
  • 2 ounces orange juice
  • 2 ounces cranberry juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Orange slice or cherry for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Pour vodka and peach schnapps over the ice..
  3. Add the orange juice and cranberry juice.
  4. Garnish with an orange slice or cherry, if desired.
  5. Serve and enjoy your drink!

Note: You can adjust the ingredient quantities to suit your taste preferences. Some variations of the Sex on the Beach recipe may include a splash of pineapple juice or a squeeze of fresh lime juice for added flavor. Feel free to experiment and make it your own! Remember to drink responsibly.
