Tutti Frutti

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries (strawberries,
    blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup diced pineapple
  • 1/2 cup diced mango
  • 1/2 cup diced papaya
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or agave syrup (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for a colder smoothie)

Instructions:

  1. Peel and slice the banana.
  2. Place the frozen mixed berries, banana slices, diced pineapple, diced mango, and diced papaya into a blender.
  3. Add the orange juice and Greek yogurt (if using) to the blender.
  4. Optionally, add a tablespoon of honey or agave syrup for added sweetness.
  5. If you prefer a colder smoothie, add a few ice cubes to the blender as well.
  6. Blend all the ingredients together until smooth and creamy. If the consistency is too thick, you can add more orange juice or water to thin it out.
  7. Once blended to your desired consistency, pour the tutti frutti smoothie into glasses.
  8. Garnish with a slice of fruit or a sprinkle of chia seeds, if desired.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing tutti frutti smoothie!

Note: Feel free to adjust the quantities of the fruits and other ingredients based on your taste preferences. You can also add other fruits like kiwi or passion fruit to further enhance the flavor. Enjoy!
