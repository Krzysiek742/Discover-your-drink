Cleansing smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 small apple, cored and chopped
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup fresh pineapple chunks
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • small piece of fresh ginger, peeled
  • 1/2 ripe avocado, peeled and pitted
  • a handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Wash the spinach thoroughly and place it in a blender.
  2. Add the cucumber, apple, banana, pineapple chunks, lemon juice, ginger, avocado and mint leaves to the blender.
  3. Pour in the coconut water.
  4. If desired, add a few ice cubes to make the smoothie colder.
  5. Blend all the ingredients until smooth and creamy. If needed, you can add more coconut water to adjust the consistency.
  6. Once blended, taste the smoothie and adjust the flavors by adding more lemon juice or ginger if desired.
  7. Pour the smoothie into a glass and enjoy it immediately.

Note: This cleansing smoothie is packed with nutrients from the fruits and vegetables, while the ginger and lemon provide a refreshing and detoxifying boost. It's best consumed in the morning on an empty stomach for maximum cleansing benefits. Remember to consult with a healthcare professional if you have any specific dietary concerns or allergies.
