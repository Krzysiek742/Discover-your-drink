Refreshing Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of fresh or frozen berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, etc.)
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 cup of coconut water or plain water
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon or lime
  • A handful of fresh mint leaves
  • 1 cup of ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Wash the berries and remove any stems or leaves.
  2. Peel the banana and break it into chunks.
  3. Place the berries, banana, coconut water, lemon or lime juice, ice cubes into a blender.
  4. Blend on high speed until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  5. Taste the smoothie and adjust the sweetness or tartness.
  6. Pour the smoothie into glasses and garnish with a sprig of mint.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing smoothie!
