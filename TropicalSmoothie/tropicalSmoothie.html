Tropical Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 60 ml tequila
  • 120 ml orange juice
  • 22.5 ml grenadine syrup
  • Ice
  • Orange slice and/or cherry, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Add the tequila and orange juice to the glass, and stir well.
  3. Slowly pour the grenadine syrup over the back of a spoon or
    a bar spoon, so that it sinks to the bottom of the glass.
  4. Garnish with an orange slice and/or cherry, if desired.
  5. Serve and enjoy your drink!

Note: For a more layered look, you can also try this method: Fill a glass with ice and pour in the tequila and orange juice. Slowly pour in the grenadine syrup over the back of a spoon, letting it sink to the bottom. Do not stir the drink, so that you have a more distinct separation of colors.
