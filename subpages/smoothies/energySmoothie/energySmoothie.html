Tequila Sunrise

Energy Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • Banana: Adds creamy texture and natural sweetness. It's also rich in potassium and fiber.
  • Spinach or kale: Adds vibrant green color and provides nutrients like iron, vitamin C, and folate.
  • Almonds or almond butter: Provides healthy fats, protein, and fiber. You can add a few almonds or a teaspoon of almond butter.
  • Greek yogurt or plant-based milk: Adds creaminess and protein.
  • Flaxseeds or chia seeds: Rich in fiber, omega-3 fatty acids, and other nutrients.

Instructions:

  1. Gather all the ingredients you'll need: a banana, spinach or kale, almonds or almond butter, Greek yogurt or plant-based milk, berry fruits, and flaxseeds or chia seeds.
  2. Peel the banana and break it into smaller chunks.
  3. If using fresh spinach or kale, wash it thoroughly. Remove any tough stems or leaves if necessary.
    Remove any tough stems or leaves if necessary.
  4. Rinse the berry fruits under cold water, if using fresh ones.
  5. Add the banana, spinach or kale, almonds or almond butter, Greek yogurt or plant-based milk, and the desired amount of berry fruits into the blender.
  6. Blend the ingredients on high speed until everything is well combined and smooth. This usually takes about 1-2 minutes, but it may vary depending on the blender's power. Enjoy your smoothie :)
