Relax Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon nut butter (such as almond or peanut butter)
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 ml of liquid CBD extract

Instructions:

  1. Place all the ingredients in a blender: sliced banana, avocado, almond milk, nut butter, honey (or maple syrup), and liquid CBD extract.
  2. Blend all the ingredients until smooth and creamy.
  3. If the smoothie is too thick, you can add a little more almond milk to achieve the desired consistency.
  4. Taste and add more honey (or maple syrup) or CBD if needed.
  5. Pour the smoothie into a glass and serve immediately.

Note: This relaxing smoothie with CBD incorporates ingredients that can help promote relaxation and reduce stress. However, it's always important to consult with a doctor or nutritionist before adding CBD to your diet to ensure it's appropriate for you and to adjust the dosage according to your needs.
