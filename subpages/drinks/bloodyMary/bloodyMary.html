Bloody Mary

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 4 ounces tomato juice
  • 1/2 ounce fresh lemon juice
  • Worcestershire sauce, to taste
  • Hot sauce, to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Celery salt, for rimming the glass, and ice

Instructions:

  1. If desired, rim the glass with celery salt by moistening the rim with a lemon wedge and dipping it into the celery salt.
  2. Fill the glass with ice cubes.
  3. In a shaker or mixing glass, combine vodka, tomato juice, fresh lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Shake or stir the mixture well to ensure all the flavors are blended.
  5. Pour the mixture into the glass over the ice.
  6. Garnish with a celery stalk, lemon wedge, olives, or pickles, as per your preference.

Note: The quantities of Worcestershire sauce, hot sauce, and seasonings can be adjusted according to your taste preferences. Some people like it spicier, while others prefer a milder version. Feel free to customize the recipe to suit your liking. Enjoy responsibly!
