Fresh Citrus Cocktail

Ingredients:

  • 200 ml orange juice
  • 100 ml grapefruit juice
  • 50 ml lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon agave syrup
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon and orange slices (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large shaker or jar, combine the orange juice, grapefruit juice, and lemon juice.
  2. Add a tablespoon of agave syrup and a few ice cubes.
  3. Close the shaker tightly and shake vigorously for a few seconds to mix the ingredients and chill the drink.
  4. Pour the mixture into a tall glass.
  5. Add a few more ice cubes to keep the drink cool.
  6. Garnish the drink with lemon and orange slices.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing taste!

Note:You can adjust the ingredient quantities according to your taste preferences. If you prefer a more citrusy flavor, you can add more lemon juice. Enjoy
