Pure Aqua Refresher

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter of water

Instructions:

  1. Take a clean bottle or pitcher.
  2. Open the tap.
  3. Pour 1 liter of water into the bottle or pitcher..
  4. Close the tap.
  5. Done! Now you can enjoy fresh, clear water.

Note:Water is an essential element of life and does not require any additional ingredients. Remember to drink an adequate amount of water each day to maintain proper hydration of your body.

Warning: Please use a glass as plastic pollutes and harms our planet.
